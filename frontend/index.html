About the Project

A final-year engineering project combining IoT, ML, and modern web technologies.

Why MilkPure AI?

Milk adulteration is a critical health hazard affecting millions globally. Common adulterants like water, urea, detergent, and synthetic compounds can cause serious health issues including kidney failure, cancer, and digestive disorders.

MilkPure AI uses a Random Forest Classifier trained on 5,000+ synthetic milk samples to detect 7 types of adulterants in under 2 seconds. The system analyzes parameters like pH, lactometer reading, fat percentage, density, conductivity, and temperature to deliver predictions with 99%+ accuracy.
