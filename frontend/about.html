About the Project

Empowering consumers with AI to ensure food safety and quality.

The Challenge

Milk adulteration is a significant health risk globally. From simple water dilution to dangerous chemicals like urea and detergent, identifying these impurities requires sophisticated equipment.

Our Solution

MilkPure AI leverages machine learning to analyze physical and chemical properties of milk samples, providing an instant safety score and adulterant identification.
